Business Account page behaviour

Switch persona and state from the top bar to test access, status, consent, verification and verified account details.

Rules covered

Access

  • Only Admins can initiate KYB.
  • Only the initiator and Org Admins can edit an in-progress form.
  • Accountants see eligible Admins, not the CTA.
  • Initiator reassignment is supported via Support.

Form

  • Full-page takeover, Alaan sidebar hidden.
  • Applicant details are basic only. AS selection is at the end.
  • Known Nymcard fields and documents are pre-filled.
  • Users can move across steps, but final submission is blocked until mandatory items are complete.

Flow coverage

Documents

  • View pre-filled and newly uploaded documents.
  • Update Nymcard carry-over with new Ruya version.
  • Delete and re-upload current version.
  • Near-expiry warning, expired hard block.

DNFPB activities

  • Six agreed activities drive shareholder threshold.
  • If selected: collect 10%+ shareholders and GoAML, AML/CFT, Compliance Officer.
  • If none: collect 25%+ shareholders only.

Consent

  • AS selected in final step from shareholders/directors or Someone else.
  • AS proof: MOA/AOA, Board Resolution, or POA.
  • Consent is always post-submission and handled by Ops.
  • Unified Awaiting Consent page.
